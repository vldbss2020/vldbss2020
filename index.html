简介

VLDB（Very Large Data Bases）中国数据库学院由VLDB基金会赞助成立，下属于中国计算机学会数据库专业委员会, 旨在为中国从事或有志于从事数据库理论与技术研究的教师、学者和研究生提供一个学习和交流的机会。学院每年不定期地邀请数据库及相关领域内国际知名的学者来中国讲学，以促进我国对国际数据库学科前沿全面及时了解，并在此前提下，立足应用研发具有特色的数据管理技术和系统。

第19届VLDB暑期学校将于2020年7月13日至7月17日举办，由山东大学计算机科学与技术学院承办，主题聚焦区块链数据管理技术中的关键问题。 主讲教授是国际上享有很高学术声誉的区块链数据库专家： 香港浸会大学教授 Jianliang Xu；华东师范大学教授 金澈清；新加坡科技设计大学助理教授 Dinh Tien Tuan Anh；新加坡国立大学高级研究员 Dumitrel Loghin。本届暑期学校还将开展形式多样的前沿讲座和研讨，为学员提供与国际知名学者的充分交流机会。

讲师简介

Prof. Jianliang Xu (Hong Kong Baptist University)


Biography:

Prof. Xu joined the Computer Science Department of Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) after he received his PhD degree in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2002. He obtained his BEng degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China in 1998. Prior to that, he received his secondary education from Suzhou High School from 1991 to 1994. Prof. Xu has been a visiting scholar at Pennsylvania State University, University Park (in Summer 2005) and a senior visiting fellow at Fudan University (in Winter 2009). He is serving as a director of the Blockchain and FinTech Lab and leads the Database Research Group at Hong Kong Baptist University.

Prof. Cheqing Jin (East China Normal University)


Biography:

Cheqing Jin, professor and vice dean of the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University. He is a member of China Computer Federation Technical Committee on Databases. His research interests include blockchain, data stream management, location-based services, uncertain data management and so on. He was the PI of several National Natural Science Foundations, and has co-authored 2 monographs and more than 100 papers. He received the first-level prize for Science and Technology of Shanghai, and the second-level prize for Science and Technology of the Ministry of education. He was guest editor of special issue “Blockchain Database Management” of Journal of Software, and PC co-chair of BlockDM 2020 workshop.

Dr. Dinh Tien Tuan Anh (Singapore University of Technology and Design)


Biography:

Dr. Dinh Tien Tuan Anh is an Assistant Professor at Singapore University of Technology and Design. His research lies at the intersection of distributed systems, databases and security. He is interested in building and applying security primitives, either based on cryptography or trusted hardware, to improve both security and performance of large scale systems. His PhD was in applying formal methods to verify security properties of P2P systems. His latest interest is centered around blockchains.

SRA. Dumitrel Loghin (National University of Singapore)


Biography:

Dumitrel Loghin received his BEng and MEng degrees from the University Politehnica of Bucharest, Romania, in 2010 and 2012, respectively, and the PhD degree in computer science from the National University of Singapore, Singapore, in 2017. He is currently a senior research fellow in the Database Systems Research Group at School of Computing, National University of Singapore. He is working under the supervision of Distinguished Professor Beng Chin Ooi. His research interests include parallel and distributed systems, embedded systems, blockchain and distributed databases, cloud and edge computing, and the time-energy performance of computer systems.
